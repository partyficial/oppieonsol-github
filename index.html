Our Story

OPPIE launched because Toly told Udi he was naming his next cat oppie. We figured out why and realized our mission. Eight community members donated funds to buy Toly his next cat. Not just any cat, a $19,000 Quantum Cat!

To make the trip home it was decided Oppie needed to get off the blockchain, real life Oppie was born.

The community raised funds to send Oppie to Canada Crypto week in Toronto. Following the success in Toronto, the community raised more funds to fly Oppie to Singapore for Token 2049 and Solana Breakpoint.

Oppie made important connections during these trips. Oppie even spoke with Iggy at Motherland Rodeo. Iggy said she loves the Quantum Cats and welcomed real life Oppie to hang out at her party. Oppie even made Iggy's own highlight video from Motherland Rodeo (16 second mark).

There were introductions for Oppie at the endless number of side events in Singapore. Oppie met Ansem, Uncle Eddie ( Hello Moon), Eric Wall (co founder Taproot Wizards), Matt Sorg ( VP of Technology at Solana) to name a few.

Oppie isn't done yet! There are trips planned for Tulum, Miami and Oakland. Who knows who Oppie will meet next!
